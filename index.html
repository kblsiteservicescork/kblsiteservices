How a survey works

Three steps from cold rooms to a clear plan.

01

The pressure test

We temporarily pressurise your home to simulate a windy winter's day. That forces hidden drafts to expose themselves so we can measure how leaky the house really is.

02

Thermal mapping & smoke tracking

Using calibrated thermal cameras and smoke sticks, we physically trace where cold air is penetrating your living spaces. You see it for yourself.

03

The clear action plan

You get an easy-to-read report showing your tradesperson, or your own DIY project, exactly what needs sealing and in what order.
