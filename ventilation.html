Ventilation validation

Build tight. Ventilate right.

Sealing a house stops the cold. But an airtight home still has to breathe, or the air inside turns stale, damp and unhealthy. We make sure yours does both.

Why sealing alone isn't the whole job

When you tighten up a house, you trap more than heat. Moisture, carbon dioxide and cooking and washing humidity all stay in unless the ventilation is doing its job. Get this wrong and you swap a cold house for a damp one.

What we check

  • Extraction fans actually move the air they claim to
  • Mechanical systems balance fresh supply against stale extract
  • Humidity and condensation are being driven out, not recycled
  • The house meets Part F of the Irish building regulations

The mould you can see is the symptom. Black mould on walls and around windows is almost always a ventilation failure, not a cleaning problem. Wipe it off and it comes back. Fix the air flow and it doesn't.

Independent, on your side

We don't sell ventilation systems, so we have no reason to recommend kit you don't need. We validate what you have, tell you plainly whether it works, and document it. For new builds, that includes the sign-off you need to certify compliance.
